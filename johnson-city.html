Johnson City

Quick Overview

Johnson City, Tennessee a suburban city located in the Northeast region of the state. As of 2022, the city has an estimated population of 72,514. Because of this population, it is the 8th largest city in the state.

Johnson City is one of three cities that makeup the well known "Tri-Cities" region within the state. The other two cities are Kingsport and Bristol. Together the Tri-Cities region makes the 5th largest region in the state.

Johnson City was part of the short-lived "State of Franklin" that lasted for 4 years.

Johnson City was incorporated in 1869.
