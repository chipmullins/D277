Nashville

Quick Overview

Nashville, Tennessee is a urban city located in the middle region of the state. Nashville serves as the capital of the state. As of 2020, the city has an estimated population of 715,884. Because of this population, it is the largest city in the state, right ahead of Memphis.

Nashville is one of the most well known cities in the world. It serves as a major entertainment hub primarily due to its influence of country music.

Nashville is located in Davidson county, but has a merged city-county government.

Nashville was incorporated in 1806.
