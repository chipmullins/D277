Knoxville

Quick Overview

Knoxville, Tennessee is a urban city located in the Eastern region of the state. Knoxville serves as a major urban center for all of East and Northeast Tennessee. As of 2020, the city has an estimated population of 190,740.This makes Knoxville the 3rd largest (and one of the fastest growing) city in the state.

Knoxville is well known for it's diverse and large arts center. It owes a lot of it's art history to the rise of bluegrass and country music in the area.

Knoxville once served as the state of Tennessee's first capital.

Knoxville was incorporated in 1815. The city also is home to the University of Tennessee
